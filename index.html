About Me

I lead multiple data science teams at Walmart who are responsible for building machine learning & optimization products. My area of expertise is NLP, Recommender Systems and Time Series forecasting.

I have a little over a decade of experience in the Data Science field and have helped build multiple machine learning algorithms to solve real world problems, deliver meaningful results and accomplish critical business objectives.
My experience spans over three continent and seven countries.

Specialties:Causal Inference, Deep Learning, Graphical Modeling, Cloud Computing,Retail Analytics, Optimization, Game Theory
